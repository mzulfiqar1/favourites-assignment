Murree, Pakistan

Murree is a city located in northern Pakistan. Visiting Murree was my first time seeing snow. The experience was great as the locals were extremely friendly and the view of the mountains from the chairlift was ethereal.

Track & Field

This is a sport that many do not consider a sport as it appears to be done with ease. However, athletes must train their muscles as well as minds for high-pressure competitions. Case in point, a false start can cause a disqualification and ruin years of work. I enjoy both watching and running track. Below are some reasons why you should start running in races:

  • Great for cardiovascular health
  • Brightens mood and inspires productivity
  • Gives incentive to eat and live healthy
  • Make friends with similar goals to you

Jigsaw Puzzles

Solving puzzles is an underrated hobby. Not only is it a fantastic alternative to scrolling on our screens, but it also develops our problem-solving and other cognitive skills. It's especially fun to solve a puzzle of an intriguing image. As well, it is a soothing activity for the mind.
